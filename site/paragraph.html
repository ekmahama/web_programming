Section 1

Recent advances in unmanned aerial vehicle sensor technology have led to a diverse range of applications for unmanned aerial vehicles. Now UAVs are increasingly used in reconnaissance, photography, agriculture, logistics, and infrastructure inspection. Research on application of UAVs for bridge inspection is receiving much attention due to the economies of scale it brings compared to traditional bridge inspection techniques. Bridges play a crucial role in transportation systems. Its reliability and availability have a direct impact on the economy. Data from the national bridge inventory shows that there are 616,096 bridges in the United States [1]. About 42 percent of these were built more than 50 years ago. Since bridges deteriorate with age, proper maintenance is required to ensure their structural integrity and safety. Therefore, regular inspection is needed in order to draw a proper maintenance plan.
